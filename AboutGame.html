What is Blades in the Dark?

What is it?

Blades in the dark is a gritty, crime fantasy RPG inspired by works of fiction such as Peaky Blinders. Set in the haunted city of Duskvol, you and members of your crew will have to fight and outwit the other criminal gangs of the seedy undercity to survive and cut your teeth, all while avoiding capture by the corrupt police force known as the Bluecoats. Will your crew come out on top or will they fail?

How many people can play?

Blades in the dark can be played with 2 to 4 players and 1 game master. The game master is responsible for setting the action and dealing out consequences, while the players work to overcome obstacles and grow their crew into a powerful criminal organization.

What sets this game apart?

Aside from the unique setting, Blades in the Dark features mechanics that make for effecient and exciting story telling. Fail forward mechanics ensure that the pace never stops and that there is always tension. Mechanics like flashblacks provide an interesting solution for groups who take too long to plan. Did your crew plan for this setback? Of course we did, let me tell you about it in this flashback.
